## Pro Tips

1. **Don't add all at once** - Start with 5-10 and see what's valuable
2. **Test feeds** - Some may be slow or inactive
3. **Regional focus** - Add local logistics news if relevant to your market
4. **Competitor tracking** - Follow major logistics companies' blogs
5. **Customer industries** - Add feeds from industries you serve (retail, manufacturing, etc.)

## Important Limitations

- Some feeds may require CORS proxy
- Reddit feeds are built-in (no need to add manually)
- Twitter via Nitter may have delays
- Always respect website ToS

## Custom Feeds

If you have specific websites you want to follow:
1. Look for RSS icon on their site
2. Check `/feed`, `/rss`, or `/atom.xml` endpoints
3. Use browser extension "Get RSS Feed URL" to find hidden feeds
4. Many WordPress/Ghost blogs auto-generate RSS at `/feed`
